
## Type	Magic number	Extension	Colors

Name | ASCII | Binary | COLOR
------------- | ----- | -----| -------------

Portable Gray Map |	P2 |	P5 |	.pgm	0–255 (gray scale) |
Portable Pix Map |	P3 |	P6 |	.ppm	0–255 (RGB) |
